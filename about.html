Who am I

Hello, I am a native speaker of Brazilian Portuguese who was born and raised in Ceará, a state in the northeast of the country. Passionate about reading, I grew up surrounded by books on philosophy, science, history, and with a lot of influence from cultures of different countries, a factor that contributed significantly to my education and to the choice of the translation profession.

Enthusiast of language studies. Currently with a degree in English Language in progress, an opportunity that made me develop the necessary skills to provide a high quality translation in the market of subtitling, business and marketing translation. I have been working with translation from English to Portuguese for 3 years, helping organizations to reach an international audience and share their content in the digital world.
